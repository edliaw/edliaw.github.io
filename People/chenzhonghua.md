# 陈中华 Chén Zhōnghuá

My teacher, [Chen Zhonghua](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chen_Zhonghua), is a nineteenth generation master of Chen-style Taijiquan.
His teachers were [[hongjunsheng]] and [[fengzhiqiang]].
