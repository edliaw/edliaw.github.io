一路 Yīlù

Lit. first road, it is the first of two form routines in Chen-style Taijiquan.

Links to this page
  • 金刚捣碓 Jīn Gāng Dǎo Duì

    Buddha’s warrior attendant pounds mortar is moves 1, 5, 13, and 81 in the 一路 Yīlù. It is moves 1, 13, and 64 in the 二路 Èrlù.

  • 野马分鬃 Yě Mǎ Fēn Zōng

    Part the wild horse’s mane is posture 47 in the 一路 Yīlù. The 3rd, 4th, and 5th move are also practiced as a jibengong exercise: pole shaking.

  • 白鹤亮翅 Bái Hè Liàng Chì

    White crane spreads its wings is moves 6, 22, and 63 in the 一路 Yīlù.

  • 斜行拗步 Xié Xíng Ǎo Bù

    Walk obliquely in twist steps is the 9th move in the 一路 Yīlù.

  • 搂膝拗步 Lǒu Xī Ǎo Bù

    Brush the knee in oblique stance is moves 7, 23, and 64 in the 一路 Yīlù. It is move 9 in the 二路 Èrlù.

  • 掩手肱捶 Yǎn Shǒu Hōng Chuí

    Fist of covering the hand is moves 12, 17, 25, 40, and 66 in the 一路 Yīlù, and moves 34, 44, 46, 50, and 58 in the 二路 Èrlù.

  • 拦擦衣 Lán Cā Yī

    Block touching coat is moves 2, 26, 52, and 67 in the 一路 Yīlù. It is move 2 in the 二路 Èrlù.

  • 庇身捶 Pì Shēn Chuí

    Fist draping over body is move 15 in the 一路 Yīlù and also move 15 in the 二路 Èrlù.

  • 单鞭 Dān Biān

    Single whip is moves 4, 28, 44, 49, 54, 68, and 75 in the 一路 Yīlù. It is move 4 in the 二路 Èrlù.

  • 初收 Chū Shōu

    First closing is move 8 in the 一路 Yīlù.

  • 再收 Zài Shōu

    Second closing is move 10 in the 一路 Yīlù.

  • 六封四闭 Liù Fēng Sì Bì

    Six sealing and four closing is moves 3, 27, 48, 53, 67, and 74 in the 一路 Yīlù and a foundational exercise of Practical Method. It is move 3 in the 二路 Èrlù.

  • 二路 Èrlù

    In relation to the 一路 Yīlù, the analogy is: the yilu lays the tracks, and the erlu puts the train on the tracks.
